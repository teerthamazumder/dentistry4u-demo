Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)

The federal CDCP helps Canadians without private dental insurance access affordable care.

Who Qualifies?
  • No private dental insurance through employer, school, or association
  • Adjusted family net income (AFNI) under $90,000/year
  • Canadian resident who has filed taxes for the previous year
  • All ages now eligible (as of 2025)
Co-Payment Breakdown
  • Under $70,000: 100% covered (no co-pay)
  • $70,000–$79,999: 60% covered (40% co-pay)
  • $80,000–$89,999: 40% covered (60% co-pay)
  • $90,000+: Not eligible

Apply online at canada.ca/dental or call Service Canada at 1-833-537-4342. We're happy to help you navigate this!
